Vanilla flavored curd cream desert

Description

Tasty vanilla desert without baking for hot afternoons. Prepared in 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Curd cheese 250g
  • Heavy cream 200ml
  • 2 packs of vanilla sugar (14g/pack)
  • Cocoa powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Whip heavy cream until it is thick and fluffy
  2. Mix whipped heavy cream with curd cheese and vanilla sugar
  3. If you want, you can divide mixture into halves and add cocoa powder in one of them
  4. Put the mixture into glass and let it chill in the fridge for 1 hour
