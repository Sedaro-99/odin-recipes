Garlic Potatoes

Description

Tasty baked garlic potatoes. Not exactly healthy but made in only 30 minutes with minimum oil.

Ingredients

  • 750g of potatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbs of sunflower oil
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Clean potatoes with water (no need to peal) and cut them to up most 0.5cm slices
  2. Peal 2 cloves of garlic and cut them to thin slices
  3. Mix the cut potatoes and garlic with 2 tbs of sunflower oil and salt
  4. Place the mixture evenly inside air fryer and bake for 30 minutes at 180°
  5. Mix every 10 minutes so its evenly fried
